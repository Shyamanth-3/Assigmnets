HYDERABAD

Hyderabad, the capital of Telangana, is known for its rich history, iconic landmarks like Charminar, and vibrant culture. The city blends Mughal heritage with modern infrastructure and a booming IT industry.





Famous for its delicious Hyderabadi Biryani, pearls, and bustling markets, Hyderabad is also home to HITEC City, making it a major hub for technology, innovation, and global enterprises.
